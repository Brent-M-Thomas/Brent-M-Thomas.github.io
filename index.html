Slide Puzzle

Final Project

The classic slide puzzle. This app lets you enter an image url and creates a slide puzzle out of it. The image is broken down into nine tiles, then the game removes one, shuffles the remaining tiles and places them on a grid. The objective is to put the tiles in order to complete the image. Clicking any tile adjacent to the empty spot will move the tile. When all the tiles are in their correct place the game is won.
